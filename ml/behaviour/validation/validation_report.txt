## 15. WHY AUC = 0.9732 -- LEAKAGE DEMONSTRATION
================================================================================

   ROOT CAUSE: computeRecurringPaymentDiscipline (featureEngineering.py:164-171)
   checks `loan.status in (B, D)` and subtracts 80 points from the discipline score.
   Since loan status B/D *is* the label (label=1), this feature directly encodes
   the target. financial_stability_index (25% weighted from discipline) is also
   contaminated by transitivity.

   Evidence:
   - recurring_payment_discipline has IV = 0.0001
     (low IV because the leaky loan-status check happens to all B/D accounts identically)
   - financial_stability_index has IV = 3.8209
     (massively inflated: >0.5 is suspicious, >3.0 is near-certain leakage)

   The discipline feature's direct leakage is partially masked by the WOE binning
   (only 1 bin for discipline = low IV) but the FSI composite amplifies it.

   REMEDIATION: Remove the leaky loan-status check from
   computeRecurringPaymentDiscipline (lines 164-171) and retrain.
   Below we simulate this by dropping the 2 contaminated features.
